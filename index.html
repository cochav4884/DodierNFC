“Building a more connected world through reliable contactless NFC technology.”

Near Field Communication (NFC) is a low-cost, contactless technology operating at 13.56 MHz with an ultra-short range and data rates up to 1.7 Mbit/s.

Its ultra-short range, low-latency connection, and ability to power one side of the link make NFC ideal for secure, instant interactions.

NFC supports both data and power exchange, enabling applications in payments, access control, smart devices, and more.
